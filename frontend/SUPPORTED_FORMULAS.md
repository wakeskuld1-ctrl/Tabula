# Supported Formulas

The Grid supports a wide range of Excel-compatible formulas powered by HyperFormula. Below are the commonly used functions categorized by type.

## Math & Trigonometry
- **Basic**: `SUM`, `PRODUCT`, `QUOTIENT`, `MOD`, `ABS`, `SIGN`, `SQRT`, `POWER`, `EXP`
- **Rounding**: `ROUND`, `ROUNDUP`, `ROUNDDOWN`, `INT`, `TRUNC`, `CEILING`, `FLOOR`
- **Trigonometry**: `SIN`, `COS`, `TAN`, `ASIN`, `ACOS`, `ATAN`, `PI`, `DEGREES`, `RADIANS`
- **Other**: `RAND`, `RANDBETWEEN`, `ROMAN`, `ARABIC`

## Statistical
- **Counting**: 
  - `COUNT`: Counts numbers only.
  - `COUNTA`: Counts non-empty cells (numbers, text, logicals).
  - `COUNTBLANK`: Counts empty cells.
  - `COUNTIF`, `COUNTIFS`: Counts cells matching criteria.
- **Aggregation**: `AVERAGE`, `AVERAGEA`, `MAX`, `MIN`, `MEDIAN`, `MODE`
- **Variance/Deviation**: `VAR`, `STDEV`, `VAR.P`, `STDEV.P`

## Text
- **Manipulation**: `CONCATENATE`, `LEFT`, `RIGHT`, `MID`, `LEN`, `TRIM`, `LOWER`, `UPPER`, `PROPER`
- **Search/Replace**: `FIND`, `SEARCH`, `SUBSTITUTE`, `REPLACE`
- **Other**: `TEXT`, `REPT`, `CHAR`, `CODE`, `EXACT`

## Logical
- **Conditional**: `IF`, `IFS`, `SWITCH`, `CHOOSE`
- **Boolean**: `AND`, `OR`, `NOT`, `XOR`, `TRUE`, `FALSE`
- **Error Handling**: `IFERROR`, `IFNA`

## Date & Time
- **Current**: `NOW`, `TODAY`
- **Creation**: `DATE`, `TIME`
- **Extraction**: `YEAR`, `MONTH`, `DAY`, `HOUR`, `MINUTE`, `SECOND`
- **Calculation**: `DATEDIF`, `DAYS`, `EDATE`, `EOMONTH`, `WEEKDAY`, `WEEKNUM`

## Lookup & Reference
- `VLOOKUP`, `HLOOKUP`
- `MATCH`, `INDEX`
- `XLOOKUP`
- `COLUMN`, `ROW`, `COLUMNS`, `ROWS`
- `HYPERLINK`

## Financial
- `PMT`, `IPMT`, `PPMT`
- `FV`, `PV`, `NPV`, `XNPV`
- `RATE`, `IRR`, `MIRR`

*Note: This is not an exhaustive list. Most standard Excel functions are supported.*
